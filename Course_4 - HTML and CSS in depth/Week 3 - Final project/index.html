SUMMER FIESTA!

We're excited to announce the latest addition to our menu - the Tuscan Truffle Tagliatelle! It's a heavenly creation by our talented Chef Gianni, who has brought his Italian roots to the table with this amazing new dish.

Our Tuscan Truffle Tagliatelle is a handcrafted pasta dish, featuring fresh, homemade tagliatelle pasta tossed with a creamy white truffle sauce. Every bite is a delightful fusion of rich, earthy truffles, parmesan cheese, and a hint of fresh parsley, delivering a truly unforgettable dining experience.

We source our truffles from the heart of Italy, ensuring an authentic, robust flavor. Combined with locally sourced, fresh ingredients, the Tuscan Truffle Tagliatelle is a testament to our commitment to provide our guests with the finest and freshest dishes.

We invite you to come and enjoy this delightful pasta dish at our restaurant. Make a reservation today and be one of the first to try the Tuscan Truffle Tagliatelle. Buon Appetito!
